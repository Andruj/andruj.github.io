About me.

Well if you scrolled all the way down here, it means you want to know more about me. That’s good right?

I am a 21 year old full stack developer, with a relentless drive for creation, design and development - and a passion for collaboration.

I love the outdoors. I am climbing in the fall, skiing in the winter, mountain biking in the spring and windsurfing in the summer. Somewhere in there I find time to hike! That might seem odd for someone who loves working in front of a computer. But I think my passions stem from a love with interacting in the world around us. I really believe that software can have the ability to impact and enhance the way we interact with the world, and that inspires me.

I won’t be available for any work or contracts until June 2016, however don’t hesitate to reach out - who knows? If you are curious about what I do in my free time, check out my Github page.
